Most LOs are producing their first video within 24 hours of signing up. Full onboarding for teams takes about a week.

No. The teleprompter walks you through block by block. And if you'd rather not record at all, your AI Avatar does it for you.

Compliance is the foundation of WOWMi, not an afterthought. Every script is scanned for RESPA, TILA, and fair lending language before you record. Enterprise teams can set custom guardrails. We'd love to walk your legal team through it on a call.
Does the AI Avatar actually look like me?
Yes. It captures your voice, face, and mannerisms from a short setup session. Most people can't tell the difference between an avatar video and a recorded one.

You get it automatically. That's the whole point.
How is WOWMi different from ChatGPT or HeyGen?
Those are tools. WOWMi is a platform. We orchestrate every major AI model — including HeyGen and OpenAI — and build the workflow, the compliance layer, and the mortgage training around them. You don't need to learn any of them.
